Unsub for Gmail

Clean up your inbox by unsubscribing from newsletters with one click.

📧 Smart Scan

Automatically finds all newsletters in your Gmail inbox using RFC 8058 unsubscribe headers.

⚡ One-Click Unsubscribe

Unsubscribe via POST request, email, or manual link — whatever the sender supports.

🗂️ Auto Archive & Filter

Automatically archive existing newsletters and create filters to skip future emails.

🌍 Multi-language

Full support for Slovak, Czech, and English languages.
